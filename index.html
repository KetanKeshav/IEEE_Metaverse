Hack-the-Metaverse 2023

Call for participation

Metaverse is a realm of new opportunities, both for technology and business!

It is an innovayive way to leverage the combined power of multiple cutting edge technologies to deliver value to people and business.

Technologies such as AR/VR/Mixed Reality; Sensing and Internet-of-Thins; 5G, Cloud Computing, Blockchain (and any other means of ultra low latency communications and computing) have the potential to transform the manner in which people interact with the physical world; especially in Healthcare; Education; Travel and Tourism; Gaming; Industrial and Enterprise applications.

The IEEE Computer Society Bangalore Chapter "Hack-the-Metaverse" is the first of it's kind platform for students to experience the real deal: a week long deep-dive Metaverse journey to understand the technology and applications; receive tutorials and training; take part in problem solving and idea demonstration via a hack-a-thon competition; and project showcase to industry professionals.

We invite you to submite your entries to the "Metaverse Hack-a-thon". The proposal should confirm to the following guidelines

  1. The proposal should be written in a single column style/format and must not exceed 2 pages.
  2. The proposal should contain three points:
    1. What is the problem statment?
    2. Why is it an important problem to solve?
    3. How will you solve it? What is your proposed solution and approach?
    4. What resources (hardware/software) do you have to solve the problem?
    5. Team composition?
      1. Team members (max of 3) + 1 mentor (preferably your insitute faculty)
      2. It is mandatory that atleast one of the team member is a woman
      3. All of the team members should be IEEE/IEEE CS members
      4. Mention: <Name; Institute; Email ID; IEEE/IEEE CS membership number> of each of the team members
      5. Who is the Team Lead?
  3. The proposal should be submitted as a pdf via the following submission portal: CMT Link
  4. Team should make their submissions under any of the following Metaverse application categories:
    • Healthcare
    • Education and training
    • Travel and Tourism
    • Industrial and Enterprise
